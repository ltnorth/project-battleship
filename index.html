Battleships

Welcome to Battleships! A guessing game where the aim is to sink your opponent's fleet before they sink yours. Take shots by clicking on the blank grid, if you hit any of your opponent's ships, you may take another shot, if not, it's your opponent's turn to take a shot at your ships!

When you are ready, click the button below to begin where player 1 has the first go.
